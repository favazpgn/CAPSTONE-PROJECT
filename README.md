# üéÆ PlayStation Sales and Ratings Analysis (PS3, PS4, & PS5)

This project is a final course assignment analyzing sales, ratings, and market trends for PlayStation games across the PS3, PS4, and PS5 generations.

---

## üéØ Project Goal

To find out what drives a game to be commercially successful on PlayStation, including analyzing regional performance and the influence of critic and user ratings on sales.

## üìà Key Findings

* **PS4 was the Sales Peak:** The PS4 era generated the highest sales volume recorded in this dataset.
* **North America is Key:** **North America (NA)** is the largest market for PlayStation games.
* **Ratings vs. Sales:** User and critic scores show a **weak connection to high sales**.
* **Real Success Driver:** **Player engagement (the number of ratings)** and strong **franchise brands** are the best predictors of high sales.
* **Top Genre:** **Action** games are the most common and successful genre.
